About Serengeti National Park

The Serengeti, located in northern Tanzania, is one of the most famous safari destinations in the world. It’s home to the legendary Great Migration, where millions of wildebeests and zebras roam freely across golden savannahs, followed by predators in one of nature’s greatest dramas.

With breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and luxurious lodges, Serengeti National Park offers the ultimate African safari experience — where every sunrise brings a new adventure.
