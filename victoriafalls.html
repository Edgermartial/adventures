About Victoria Falls

Known locally as "Mosi-oa-Tunya" — the Smoke That Thunders — Victoria Falls is one of the largest and most breathtaking waterfalls on Earth. Located on the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe, it offers awe-inspiring views, thrilling activities, and luxurious lodges along the Zambezi River.

Whether you’re chasing adventure through white-water rafting and bungee jumping or seeking serene river cruises at sunset, Victoria Falls is a destination that captivates every traveler’s heart.
