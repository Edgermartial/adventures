About Amboseli National Park

Amboseli National Park is one of Kenya’s most iconic safari destinations, renowned for its large herds of elephants and views of Mount Kilimanjaro.

Experience rich wildlife, vibrant Maasai culture, and some of the most breathtaking sunsets in Africa.
